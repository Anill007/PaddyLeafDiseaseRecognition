PADDY LEAF DISEASE CLASSIFICATION USING CNN

Paddy is rice that still has its hull surrounding the inner edible kernel. While most rice is prepared for consumption or further processing after the hull has been removed, a few rice products use paddy as the starting material and then remove the hull during processing.

The rice is the meal crop and required for the purpose of preparation of various dishes. The rice is the main source of carbohydrates in the diet. The paddy is grown floodplains and coastal regions as it requires appreciable amount of water.

Plant disease affect the growth and crop yield of the plants and make social, ecological and economic impacts on agriculture. Plant leaf diseases also cause significant economic losses of farmers. Early detection of the diseases deserve special attention.

About Project

This is a web based application that detects the disease of the paddy leaf and gives the information about the leaf to the user. For this, we are relying on Convolution Neural Network (CNN) to classify healthy and diseased paddy leaves.
